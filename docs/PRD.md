# Product Requirements Document — Ultimate Workspace MVP

## Vision

AI-powered productivity workspace combining Notion, Todoist, Motion, and Islamic prayer-aware scheduling. The Smart Routine Engine is the flagship feature.

## Target Users

- Software engineers and knowledge workers
- Muslim professionals requiring prayer-aware scheduling
- Freelancers managing multiple work streams

## MVP Scope

### In Scope
- Auth (Google, GitHub, email credentials)
- Onboarding with profession-based routine seeding
- Smart Routine Engine (weekly/daily views, prayer integration, analytics, AI optimizer)
- Tasks with auto-scheduling
- Encrypted vault
- Smart Routine Engine with AI optimizer

### Out of Scope (Phase 2)
- Habits, Notes, Calendar sync, Goals, Push notifications, Passkeys

## Success Metrics
- Onboarding completion rate > 80%
- Daily routine view engagement
- Task completion rate improvement week-over-week
- Prayer block consistency tracking
